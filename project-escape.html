About This Game

《逃離地獄邊緣》是一款以中世紀修道院為背景的懸疑冒險遊戲。在遊戲中，你將與主角艾德琳一同踏入一個迷人且充滿宗教元素的中世紀世界，與你的夥伴海雷娜一同探索並逃離這座修道院。 圍繞著神與人，自由與信仰的故事，由此開啟——

Escape from the Cursed Convent is a narrative-driven adventure game set in a medieval girls' convent, offering a rich storytelling experience, and several gameplay styles including QTE, hunting, cooking, and puzzle-solving.

專案經歷

Project Experience

很高興能參與《逃離地獄邊緣》的製作，並有機會分享這段經歷！ 我在專案中主要負責劇情 CG、道具與地圖繪製，使用 Procreate、Clip Studio Paint 與 Photoshop 交替進行創作。開發期間，我與遊戲設計師密切合作，整合需求並在時限內完成各項任務，過程中積極溝通與調整，確保視覺風格與遊戲功能一致，並符合專案目標。 這是我首次參與完整上架的遊戲製作，從中獲得了寶貴的實務經驗，也更了解團隊協作與遊戲製作流程。

I'm excited to have participated in the development of Escape from the Cursed Convent, and to be able to share the results of our work! I was mainly responsible for story CGs, props, and map illustrations, using Procreate, Clip Studio Paint, and Photoshop throughout the creative process. During development, I worked closely with the game designer to interpret and integrate design requirements, delivering assets within deadlines while maintaining consistent style and functionality. This was my first experience working on a game that was officially released, and it provided me with valuable insights into real production workflows and team collaboration.
