About This Game

布偶之心是一款繪本風格的 2D 角色扮演遊戲，玩家將扮演小布偶，在村莊中協助村民完成任務以確保宴會順利舉行。 遊戲內含兩種小遊戲：機關迷宮與閃避障礙。

Puppet's Heart is a 2D role-playing game with a storybook art style. Players take on the role of a small doll who helps villagers complete tasks to ensure the festival can be held successfully. The game features two mini-games: a puzzle maze and an obstacle-dodging challenge.

專案經歷

Project Experience

專案初期，我和隊友一起討論遊戲的核心概念，由我負責概念草圖的繪製與後續關卡設計。同時，我也負責大部分的美術與程式開發，並嘗試使用 GarageBand 製作音效。

At the beginning of the project, I worked with my teammates to define the concept of the game. I was responsible for creating concept sketches and designing the levels. I also handled most of the artwork and programming, and experimented with making sound effects using GarageBand.

程式方面，我使用 Inky 撰寫對話腳本並導入 Unity，結合 Animator 與 Particle System 製作遊戲開場動畫，並運用 URP 調整遊戲內的色調與光效，也初步學習了 Cinemachine 的運用。 在這個過程中，我也學會了 Unity 的物件導向架構與腳本生命周期等基礎概念，對我之後的學習有很大幫助。

On the programming side, I used Inky to write the dialogue scripts and integrated them into Unity. I used Animator and Particle System to create the game's opening animation, applied URP for color tone and lighting adjustments, and also began learning the basics of Cinemachine. Through this experience, I gained a solid understanding of Unity's object-oriented structure, script lifecycle and more, which greatly helped me in later projects.

美術方面，初期嘗試以寫實、手繪、童話風格呈現，但後期因應時程與人力需求，改以簡單線條與色塊為主，確保風格統一並順利完成。

In terms of art, I initially explored a realistic, hand-drawn, storybook style. However, due to time and team constraints, I later shifted to a simpler style using clean lines and flat color to ensure consistency and timely completion.

這是我第一次開發遊戲，幾乎是從零開始學習，因此對我影響深遠，讓我完整理解一款遊戲從發想到實際執行檔輸出的完整流程。

This was my first time developing a game, and I had to learn almost everything from scratch. It had a profound impact on me and gave me a comprehensive understanding of the full development process — from concept to executable build.
