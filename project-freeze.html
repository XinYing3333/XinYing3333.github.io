About This Game

泡泡派對！是一款雙人對戰遊戲，兩位玩家分別扮演髒污和清潔，在場地中通過踩踏占領地盤,在時限内占領區域更多的玩家獲勝。

Bubble Party! is a two-player party game where players take on the roles of Polute and Clean. Players compete to claim territory by stepping on tiles. The one who controls the most area within the time limit wins the match.

GGJ是什麽？

What is GGJ?

GGJ（Global Game Jam）是遊戲開發界一年一度的全球性活動。參與者需在48小時內完成組隊並開發一款遊戲。活動會在全球多個地點同步進行，並使用同一個主題進行創作。詳情可參考活動官網。

GGJ (Global Game Jam) is an annual global event in the game development community. Participants are challenged to form teams and create a complete game within 48 hours. The jam is held simultaneously across the world, and all teams create games based on a shared theme.More details available at the official website.

我學到了

What I Learned

這是我參與 GGJ 2025 時的專案作品。在極短時間內，與完全陌生的隊友開始協作並完成一個完整的遊戲專案，對我來說是一次非常有趣且充實的經驗。

透過這次機會，我不僅與更有經驗的業界人士交流、請教學習，也在團隊中負責遊戲美術，並學習了Git分支管理的實作方式。在實際的多人版本控制流程中，我嘗試了 pull 與 push 自己負責的內容，這對我未來參與專案開發和團隊協作有很大幫助。

This project was created during Global Game Jam 2025, where I collaborated with new teammates to develop a two-player competitive game within 48 hours. I was in charge of the game’s art and also learned to use Git for version control. I practiced branching, pulling, and pushing my work in a real collaborative environment — a valuable experience that helped me improve both technically and in teamwork.
